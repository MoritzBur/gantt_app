# Notes and thinking

Plans rarely fail because the bars are wrong. They fail because the context lives somewhere else.

![Note tabs and panel header](./_images/tabs.png)

The notes panel is on the right side of the screen. This screenshot is only showing the tab strip and note header on purpose:

- tabs at the top keep multiple notes open
- the next note explains the editor area
- the note after that explains the related-notes area

Tab behavior to remember:

- a normal preview can be replaced by the next preview you open
- double-click a tab to pin it permanently
- `Ctrl/Cmd + W` closes the active note tab
- `Ctrl/Cmd + Tab` switches to the next note tab
- `Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + Tab` switches to the previous note tab
- `Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + Left/Right` also cycles through note tabs
- `Esc` collapses the notes panel when the editor itself is not focused

Notes keep the why next to the schedule:

- assumptions
- checklists
- meeting conclusions
- related files
