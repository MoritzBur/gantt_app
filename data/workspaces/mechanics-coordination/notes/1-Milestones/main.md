## Core question
Can a morphing single-slotted flap reduce peak gust loads on a regional turboprop wing without sacrificing low-speed lift margin?

## Success definition
- Keep peak hinge moment increase below 3%
- Reduce root bending moment in design gust cases by at least 8%
- Demonstrate agreement between CFD and tunnel trends for the chosen schedule family
