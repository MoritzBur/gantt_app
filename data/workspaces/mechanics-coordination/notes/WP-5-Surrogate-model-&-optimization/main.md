# Surrogate and optimization

## Why a surrogate
Full unsteady CFD is too expensive for broad schedule exploration, so the thesis needs a fast approximation that still respects the validated trend directions.

## Planned inputs
- Gust amplitude and reduced frequency
- Baseline flap angle
- Morphing schedule timing and amplitude

## Planned outputs
- Peak root bending moment
- Peak hinge moment
- Integrated lift loss penalty

## Acceptance criteria
- Cross-validation error below the noise floor of the tunnel repeats
- Correct ranking for the top five candidate schedules
