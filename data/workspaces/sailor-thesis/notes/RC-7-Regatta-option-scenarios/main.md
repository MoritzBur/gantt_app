# Regatta option scenarios

Leon is a professional sailor and yacht racing coach who is also finishing a master's thesis.

This startup view is for one specific question:

- How much uninterrupted thesis time is left once his fixed personal commitments are accounted for?
- What happens if he decides to add one of the regatta options from the orange calendar?

These are not confirmed commitments yet.

Leon keeps them in a separate calendar so he can turn them on one by one and see how each campaign would affect the thesis timeline.

Recommended thought process:

- Keep the fixed coaching weekends active.
- Toggle one regatta option at a time.
- Watch which thesis phases still have a long uninterrupted block for deep work.

This demo intentionally does not use asset management. It is about personal calendar context and scenario planning.
