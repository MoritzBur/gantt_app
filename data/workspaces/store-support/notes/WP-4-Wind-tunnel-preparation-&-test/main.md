# Tunnel campaign

## Goal
Generate a compact but credible dataset that tests whether the adaptive schedules predicted by CFD survive contact with reality.

## Test assets
- Instrumented flap section with interchangeable trailing-edge inserts
- Pressure taps around the flap shoulder
- Six-component balance plus hinge moment measurement

## Campaign philosophy
- Short matrix, repeatable runs
- Prioritize clean comparisons over maximum coverage
- Keep one day of margin for re-runs and instrumentation drift checks

## Things to watch
- Sensor zero drift after lunch restart
- Repeatability at high flap deflection
- Turnaround time between schedule variants
