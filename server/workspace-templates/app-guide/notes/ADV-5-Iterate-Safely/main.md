# Iterate safely

Good planning is not about getting the first version perfect.

The app is strongest when you keep cycling between:

- moving bars in the main schedule
- checking blocker impact in the toolbar
- capturing a snapshot when a plan version is worth keeping
- updating the explanation in notes

That loop lets you try an option, see the cost, and adjust without losing context.

This workspace can stay around as a manual while you do that in your real workspace.
