# Keep this workspace as your reference manual

![Guide structure in the task tree](../_images/workspace-overview.png)

You do not need to remember everything at once.

This workspace is meant to stay useful:

- come back when you forget how blockers should be used
- revisit the notes when you want to connect a new calendar
- scan the prefixed groups to jump straight to the relevant topic
- steal wording or structure for your own plans

If a feature is interesting, the task tree is your table of contents and the notes are the explanation.
